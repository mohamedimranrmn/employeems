The Employee Management System with Admin and Employee Dashboards is a comprehensive web-based solution designed to streamline and enhance the management of employee-related information within an organization. This system off ers distinct interfaces for administrators and employees, providing tailored access to functionalities and information relevant to their roles. Administrators can access employee lists, categories, salary Logs, Projects, List of Admins through the admin dashboard, while employees can view their own details such as image, salary, name, email, project and role through the employee dashboard.
